Power BI Dashboard: Supply Chain Analytics & Inventory Analysis

This portfolio highlights two Power BI projects designed to tackle real-world problems in supply chain and inventory management using a solid tech stack.

Supply Chain Analytics Dashboard: This dashboard includes a Make vs. Buy Analysis tool that combines internal manufacturing cost data with quote analysis. It calculates costs across production volumes and uses Power Query for ETL and DAX for advanced calculations. The result is a scalable and interactive tool that helps businesses make smarter supply chain decisions, going beyond what traditional spreadsheets can offer.
