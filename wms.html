Relational Database Design

In today's manufacturing landscape, the role of relational databases is recognized as fundamental to achieving operational success. At the heart of an industry where accuracy, efficiency, and decision-making are key, these databases provide an organized and effective approach for handling data that is integral to daily operations. They ensure a consistent and smooth information flow, linking critical areas such as production, logistics, and quality management. This integration allows for a comprehensive perspective of the manufacturing process, important for process optimization and minimizing inefficiencies.

In this project I designed and implemented a relational database in SQL that focuses on manufacturing needs for production and inventory management.
